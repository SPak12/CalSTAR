Project U.R.S.A.

Upright
Recovery and
Sight
Acquisition

[2016 - 2017] URSA Major was the first rocket we submitted to NASA's Student Launch. Engineered to reach an apogee of a mile, the rocket boasted a Blue Tube frame with an L motor. For our payload, we decided to go with SAGITTA-VL or Sight-Aided Ground IdenTificaTion And Vertical Landing which used a nose-facing camera inside a clear nose cone to achieve detection of ground targets after main chute deployment.
